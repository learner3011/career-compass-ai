Privacy Policy

Career Compass AI stores account information and resume-related analysis data so users can sign in, view history, access reports, and download improved resume versions.

What We Collect

  • Email address and encrypted password for account access.
  • Uploaded resume files and extracted resume text for analysis and enhancement features.
  • Generated reports, resume scores, suggestions, and enhanced resume content.
  • Basic account timestamps such as signup date and recent login time.

How We Use Data

  • To authenticate users and verify email ownership with OTP.
  • To analyze resumes against job roles and save report history.
  • To generate enhanced resume drafts and downloadable PDF reports.
  • To provide customer support and investigate technical issues.

Resume Data Handling

Uploaded resume data is kept so users can revisit previous analyses, download reports, and continue improving their resumes over time. Users should only upload resumes they have permission to use.

Data Sharing

Career Compass AI does not sell personal data. Data may be processed by third-party infrastructure providers used for hosting, database, email delivery, and AI processing.

Security

Reasonable security measures are used to protect stored account and report data. No internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.
